Cheese Burger Recipe

Description

This classic cheese burger is made with all natural ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef patty
  • Burger bun
  • Cheddar cheese slice
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices
  • Pickles

Steps

  1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Season ground beef patty with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill patty for 4-5 minutes on each side or until desired doneness is reached.
  4. During the last minute of cooking, place cheddar cheese slice on top of patty to melt.
  5. Toast burger bun on the grill for 1-2 minutes.
