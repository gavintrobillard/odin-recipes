BBQ Pizza Recipe

Description

This home made pizza is made with all natural ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • BBQ sauce
  • Cooked chicken breast, shredded
  • Mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • Cilantro, chopped

Steps

  1. Flatten and shape dough into desired shape and thickness.
  2. Using a spoon, evenly spread bbq sauce over pizza dough leaving 1/4 inch along the side of the dough.
  3. Evenly Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over bbq sauce.
  4. Add shredded chicken breast, red onion, and cilantro over the mozzarella and bbq sauce.
