Chili Recipe

Description

This hearty chili is made with a blend of spices and fresh ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Kidney beans
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Onion, chopped
  • Garlic, minced
  • Chili powder
  • Cumin
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. In a large pot, brown the ground beef over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onion and minced garlic, cook until softened.
  3. Add diced tomatoes, kidney beans, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  4. Simmer for at least 30 minutes to allow flavors to meld together.
